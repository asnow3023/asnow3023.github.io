About the Library of Ruina Universe...


Story of The Library of Ruina takes place in a post-apocalpytic universe
with limited amount of space for habitation left for humanity. It is not uncommon to witness a non-human creature in this universe, as if all fictional creatures have been magically started to exists all suddenly.



"The City"


Library of ruina takes place in "The City." Outside the city there exists non-human creatures or monsters mostly hostile to human specimen. There also exists some ancient ruins with exceptional technologies which indicates that humans once lived outside "The City" at some point in the history. Some group of treasure hunters or underlings of big corporations attempts to explore these ruins in search for ancient technologies, but only few or none return to tell stories of horrible nightmares they suffered.





In The City, human rights have fallen to the ground, and there exists less to none ethical standard on human lives. In the Backstreets and outskirts of The City, corpses of countless bodies lay on ground until the dawn sweeps by and Sweepers and Fixers clean the mess. Only a few individuals with little justice left in their heart takes arm and protects the innocent, yet the world remains unchanged in its violence and publicity of monstrousness in human character. The city has no name, and is split into 25 "Districts."





Each districts are split among "Wings," which are entitled to most impactful corporation of that district with exceptional technological singluarity. This means each 25 members of "Wings" have unique singularities that benefits humanity in any way possible. Wings of District 1, 2 and 3 acts as a governing bodies of The City, calling themselves "The Head" and controls The City itself.



Lobotomy Corporation, and the Fall


Among the twenty-five members of Wings, Lobotomy Corporation was a corporation that took lead in District 12 of The City. Lobotomy's singularity dealt with mysterous substance called "Enkepalin" to create vast amount of eco-friendly energy using the substance. It is said that Lobotomy Corporation was able to provide an enough energy for the entire city just from a single branch office. This influenced many other corporations and the growth of advancement in singularities with the energy provided by Lobotomy.





However, the trustful company that many other members of the Wings depended on suddenly disappeared in a course of seven days. For three days, the place where headquarter of Lobotomy Corporation used to exist now severed the sky with an enourmous ray of innocent light that brought dawn to the entire city for three days. After three nightless days, the city was then filled with pure darkness as if it was questioning the city when it ever shined with day's warm light. Evil-filled city with pure darkness exiled the daylight for three consecutive days, until the darknes faded away and calmed the city with unspoken silence for a single day.





Lobotomy Gone, Library Born


With the fall of Lobotomy, District 12 soon fell into chaos, and various kinds of "Distortion" began to occur within The City. From a man becoming a pianist and causing massacre to thousands of people to an mysterous case of ascension of random individual to the sky, all kinds of abnormal situations began to occur in The City after the fall of Lobotomy. And place where Lobotomy used to exist has been covered in immersive fog that prohibits anyone to go near. The center of this distortion stands a tall library. We call this The Library of Ruina.
