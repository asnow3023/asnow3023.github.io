Website Evaluations

Sasaki.com

Sasaki is a design firm that expertises in interdisciplinary architecture and planning. The firm devleoped clean and excellent design of their website to prove their design skills

  • Contrast: Shows absolutely perfect color contrast and uses it to guide viewer through all of their intended content.
  • Repetition: Repetitive use of specific or similar colors are well managed and helps the theme to stand out even more.
  • Alignment: May have differnt opinion on font sizes. Smaller font sizes would have improved the visibility of the actual content. Excellent use of content aligment.
  • Proximity: Excellent space usage between each contents. Guides overall navigation of contents.

Craigslist

Craiglist is a website used as a community to advertise personal or business sales of various merchandises split into many categories.

  • Contrast: Contrasts for font and background has significant use.
  • Repetition: Craiglist only uses blue , white, and gray which does have repitition, but lacks the efficient and optimized usage of such colors onto contents.
  • Alignment: Text alignments are used in a way it distracts the readibility.
  • Proximity: Text and content proximity inbetween distracts the readibility.
